About

Hi

I’m just like every other web developer. I have to have a website, because it proves that I can do this stuff. But doing simple stuff, like this blog is so easy it proves pretty much nothing.

The other major problem is I don’t have a lot to say, so the chance of me actually saying interesting is very limited indeed. At the very least, I want the money I spend on domains to be doing something, even if it’s dull and flat as a pancake.

I really don’t wish to jinx this spate of enthusiasm, by saying something like “I’m going to post lots of stuff” because I probably won’t.
